Monitor light bars have become an essential desk accessory for anyone who spends long hours in front of a screen. By casting a warm, directed glow across your keyboard and desk without washing out your monitor, they reduce eye strain, improve contrast, and make your workspace feel more inviting. The best ones clip directly onto your monitor top and draw power via USB, so there’s no cable mess or desk space wasted. When choosing a monitor light bar, pay attention to three key factors: beam pattern (asymmetric lighting that stops at the top edge of the screen avoids haze and glare), adjustability (color temperature from warm to cool, plus dimming), and build quality (a sturdy clamp that won’t tilt or wobble). Most models now include a remote or touch control, but placement of those controls matters, too. The picks below represent the best balance of light quality, features, and value for 2026.
