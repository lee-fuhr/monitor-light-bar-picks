Quick answer

For most people, the BenQ ScreenBar is the go-to choice: it balances light quality, build, and ease of use better than anything else on the market.

If you prefer a wireless remote and a wider light spread, the BenQ ScreenBar Halo is a premium step up; on a tight budget, the Quntis Computer Monitor Light Bar delivers surprising performance for very little money.

A good monitor light bar saves desk space, reduces eye strain, and eliminates harsh overhead glare, but choosing the right one isn’t as simple as picking the cheapest option. Compatibility with your monitor’s bezel, the quality of the light beam, color temperature options, and long-term durability all vary widely, and many models look similar online but perform very differently in practice. This site cuts through the noise to help you find a light bar that actually works for your setup and your eyes.
